The challange

What problem we chose to solve: “Make cities and communities inclusive, safe, robust and sustainable”.

Sub-goal we chose to foucs on: 11.3 By 2030, enhance inclusive and sustainable urbanization and capacity for participatory, integrated and sustainable human settlement planning and management in all countries

Solution

Many cities are in need of people who can contribute, but may not have the financial ability to help. We want to make it easier for people to volunteer. Volunteering is a great way to contribute to the community. “Volunteering does not only have a positive effect on society - people who work voluntarily are happier than others.”

Read more!

The app

With the God Gerning app you can easily find many ways to contribute. This could be by physical labor, social interaction or donations. In the app you can quickly find a category that intrigues you, that you are interested and passionate about. You find a way to contribute by choosing a category, and a deed where you can sign up to contribute. After completing you can post your good deed on the feed in the app for all your friends to see.
