The app

In the app you will be given a challenge to complete in order to ensure a good, stable mental health. The challenges will be based on recommendations from therapists, mental health experts and organizations. The challenges will differ in difficulty depending on if it’s a daily, weekly or monthly challenge. A daily challenge could be to take a 30-minute walk. By doing this you will get fresh air and exercise, which will help your overall mental (and physical) health. A weekly challenge could be to reach out to a friend. This can be challenging for many people, as many people with bad mental health shut people out. The app can be used by people with mental illness, bad mental health and/or by people who want to insure good mental health.

Log Inn

When you first open the "Health First" app, you will see the option to log in with your email address and your password, if you already have an account, but if you do not have an account there is an option to register at the bottom and you can create an account.

Home

The home page you can see three different boxes. The first box will give you a daily quote, and will be new every day. The boxes below are goals to complete, You can decide if you want daily, weekly and/or monthly goals. In the photo of the app, the person has chosen a daily and a weekly goal.

Journal

The journal page is created to track how you feel. You chose the emotion you are feeling that day, and can write about your day. Your journal is saved in the app so you can look bad at your previous days. Under is a diagram to show your mood that month. You can change it to show your weekly, monthly and yearly mood.

Contact

On the contact page you have different numbers to different mental health organizations. You can call these numbers if you need to talk to someone. If you are not comfortable speaking on the phone with someone, you can use the chat service at the bottom. It is linked to Mental Helse’s chat.

My profile

Here is your profile page. You can see your name and how many days continually you’ve been using the app. You have your phone number, email address and location. This is only visible to you. You can edit your personal information with the “edit profile” button.
