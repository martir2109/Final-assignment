Why not make it easy and social to do good deeds for your community?

The challange

A lot of people at all different ages are struggling with their mental health and are struggling to improve it. Mental health is a very important and serious topic that needs to be brought up more. Therefore we chose to have UN's Sustainable Development goal number 3, “Good health and well-being”. The goal is about ensuring healthy lives and promoting well-being for all at all ages (Nations, u.d.).

Read more!

Solution

Our solution is to make more people at all ages improve their mental health. We came up with the idea of an app that can give the users daily quotes, daily/weekly or monthly challenges, all to what the user desires.

Read more!

The app

With the God Gerning app you can easily find many ways to contribute. This could be by physical labor, social interaction or donations. In the app you can quickly find a category that intrigues you, that you are interested and passionate about. You find a way to contribute by choosing a category, and a deed where you can sign up to contribute. After completing you can post your good deed on the feed in the app for all your friends to see.
