Solution

We want to encourage good mental health in order to live a happy and healthy lifestyle

Improving mental health

There are multiple ways to improve your mental health, such as talking about your problems, eating healthy, living an active lifestyle and keeping in touch with friends and family (mentalhealth.org). We want to improve people's mental health by challenging people to do small tasks such as these in an app.

Why an app?

The reason we chose an app to solve this problem was because we live in 2020, and most people use their phone multiple hours a day. Therefore a good way to reach the people is by making an app they can download on their phone in the future.
